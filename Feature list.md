## Core Activity Tracking
- Log activities across multiple exercise types (walking, running, swimming, cycling, weights, gym equipment)
- Plan a programme of exercise in advance
- Record actual performance against planned activity
- Search and filter completed activity records
- Summary statistics and data visualisations of historical exercise data

## Competition Support
- Record upcoming races/events
- Log results of completed races
- Display personal best times and performance milestones

## Social & Group Features
- Form virtual training groups
- Compete with other users toward shared or individual goals
- Personal trainers can monitor client activity remotely
- Virtual coaching and support from trainers to clients

## Route Tracking
- Record routes taken during runs or cycles
- Display routes overlaid on a map
- Share routes with other users (with creator permission)
- Browse and discover routes shared by others

## Device Integration
- Connect and sync with fitness devices (Fitbit, GPS watches, etc.)
- Upload and import data from third-party devices
